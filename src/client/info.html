App Shorcuts:

  • Space: Play/Pause

  • Arrow Left: Previous song

  • Arrow Right: Next song

  • Arrow Up: Raise volume by 5%

  • Arrow Down: Lower volume by 5%

  • key M: Toggle mute

  • key L: Change loop state

  • key S: Toggle shuffle

You also can drag and drop one song at a time to play it
